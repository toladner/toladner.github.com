About Me

I am a PhD student at the Technical University of Munich, specializing in AI safety with a focus on the formal verification of neural networks, trustworthy AI, and applications in safety-critical systems.

A list of publications is available below. For collaboration inquiries or more information, please email me at toladner@pm.me () or visit my university profile.

I am also the administrator of CORA ‚Äî a toolbox for continuous reachability analysis ‚Äî which supports my work in formal neural network verification (and more!). CORA regularly competes in competitions involving the formal verification of neural networks (VNN-COMP, ARCH-COMP), in which I serve as both evaluation and repeatability chair. As part of my duties at the Technical University of Munich, I supervise student theses, design seminars and practical courses, as well as give lectures to students about my research. My work is supported by the project "Formal Verification of Analog AI Hardware (FAI)" funded by the German Research Foundation (Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft, DFG).
