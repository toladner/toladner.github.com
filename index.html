About Me

I am a researcher at the Technical University of Munich, specializing in AI safety with a focus on the formal verification of neural networks and cyber-physical systems. My research is dedicated to ensuring that AI systems perform reliably in safety-critical applications.

I am also the administrator of CORA — a toolbox that supports my work in neural network verification (and more!).

For collaboration inquiries or more information, please email me at tobias.ladner@tum.de or visit my university profile. I am a member of the DFG-funded project Formal Verification of Analog AI Hardware (FAI). A list of publications is available below.
