Description

Do you get bored easily from your wallpapers? This app provides you with a new, beautiful HD wallpaper every day, sourced from your local albums. Create a folder with images on your phone, select it in the app, and you will always have wonderful wallpapers!

Features

  • Easy to use
  • Configure time intervals: Get a new picture twice a day or more!
  • Add effects to your wallpaper (Blur, Saturation, Brightness)
  • Customize your sources (albums)
  • Favor images to see them again
  • Mark images as 'never show again'
  • Use different settings for wallpaper and lockscreen

All wallpapers are taken from your local albums. Create a folder with images on your phone, select it in the app, and enjoy your daily wallpapers!
